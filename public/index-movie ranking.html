The Best Movies According to Ryan

My Top 3 listed Movies of all-Time

The Matrix

I had seen this movie when I was in my
teenage years and it changed the way I
looked at movies

Marvel Cinematic Universe

These include all movies in the franchise
up to the last Avengers movie. Once the
last Avengers movie was complete, the
franchise started to go downhill

Transformers: First three

The Transformers movies, when they first
came out, were the hype of the years they
were released. But as time went on, they
started to fall off the radar.
